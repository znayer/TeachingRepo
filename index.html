Nevada and Arizona Neurology and Sleep Centers

Tri-State Neurology, located in Bullhead City, Mojave Arizona and Henderson, Nevada is the region's leading provider of comprehensive health care services.

With state of the art technology and a sincere commitment to the community, we are delivering advanced health care programs and services, distinguished by the skill and compassion of our physicians, nurses and caregivers.

Our Services Diagnostic Testing

We offer a variety of services, as listed below:

  • EEG(electroencephlagram)
  • Video EEG (24-48 hrs)
  • NCV/EMG (Nerve Conduction Study)
  • DBS Programming (Deep Brain Stimulation)
  • VNS Programming (Vagus Nerve Stimulation)
  • Overnight Polysomnogram (OPSG)
  • Split Night Study
  • C-PAP/BI-PAP Titration
  • MWT (Maintenance of Wakefulness Test)
  • MSLT (Multiple Sleep Latency Test)
  • Balance Testing
  • Autonomic Nervous System Testing
  • Botox Injection
