How We Help Our Services

Neurology Clinical

A neurologist is a physician specializing in neurology and is trained to investigate and treat neurological disorders. This can range from migraines to dementia.

Neurophisiology

Clinical neurophysiology is a medical specialty that studies the central and peripheral nervous systems through the recording of the bioelectrical activity, whether spontaneous or stimulated. It encompasses both research regarding the pathophysiology along with clinical methods used to diagnose diseases involving both central and peripheral nervous systems.

Sleep Medicine

Sleep medicine is a medical specialty or subspecialty devoted to the diagnosis and therapy of sleep disturbances and disorders. Excessive snoring, or not getting restful sleep, can all be analyzed and treated in our sleep centers.
